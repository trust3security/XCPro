## Water Ballast System

### Tank Configuration
| Tank Location | Capacity (liters) | Notes |
|---------------|------------------|-------|
| Inner wing tanks (2) | 60 each | Filled first |
| Middle wing tanks (2) | 29 each | Filled second |
| Outer wing tanks (2) | - | 15m tips filled from wingtip |
| Fin tank (optional) | 7.8 | Trim ballast |
| **Total Capacity** | **178-186** | Depending on configuration |

### Ballast Features
- Three tanks per wing for optimal weight distribution
- Outer tanks filled from wing tips
- Electric ballast valve control (Performance Edition)
- Dump time: Approximately 3-4 minutes
- Integrated 'bug-wiper garages' in fuselage

## Engine Options

### Ventus 3T - Turbo Sustainer
- **Engine:** Solo 2350
- **Power:** 18.7 kW (25 hp)
- **Climb Rate:** Up to 0.9 m/s (177 ft/min)
- **Weight Penalty:** ~20 kg

### Ventus 3M - Self-Launcher
- **Engine:** Solo 2625-01 or 2626-01i Neo
- **Power:** 45 kW (60 hp)
- **Climb Rate:** >3.0 m/s (590 ft/min)
- **Features:** Automatic redundancy system (Neo version)
- **Weight Penalty:** ~35 kg

### Ventus 3E - Electric Self-Launcher
- **Motor:** Solo electric
- **Power:** 39 kW (53 hp) peak at 2900 rpm
- **Batteries:** Two EMRAX 400V batteries
- **System Weight:** 77 kg total
- **Climb to:** ~2,500 m (8,200 ft)
- **Features:** Emission-free, low noise

### Ventus 3F - Front Electric Sustainer (FES)
- **Type:** Front-mounted electric propeller
- **Retractable:** Yes
- **Weight Penalty:** Minimal
- **Advantage:** Better CG position

## Fuselage Options

### Sport Edition
- **Features:** Essential competition-focused design
- **Cockpit:** Sporty, ergonomic layout
- **Weight:** Lighter (285 kg empty)
- **Target:** Competition pilots
- **Self-launch:** Now available with electric (3E)

### Performance Edition
- **Features:** Upscale comfort and equipment
- **Cockpit:** Front-opening canopy, more spacious
- **Weight:** Heavier (295 kg empty)

#### Example for 18m at 450kg:
```
V1: 88 km/h   W1: 0.58 m/s
```

#### Example for 15m at 440kg:
```
V1: 87 km/h   W1: 0.62 m/s
V2: 102 km/h  W2: 0.68 m/s
V3: 170 km/h  W3: 1.55 m/s
```

### Required LX Settings
1. **Glider Empty Weight:** 285-315 kg (depending on configuration)
2. **Pilot Weight:** Your actual weight with equipment
3. **Water Ballast:** 0-186 liters
4. **Wing Area:** 10.96 m^2 (18m) or 9.66 m^2 (15m)
5. **Bugs Factor:** 0-20% degradation

### MacCready Settings
- Weak conditions: 0.5-1.0 m/s
- Moderate conditions: 1.0-2.5 m/s
- Good conditions: 2.5-3.5 m/s
- Strong conditions: 3.5+ m/s

## Handling Characteristics

### Positive Attributes
- **Remarkable maneuverability** from first aerotow
- **Immediate and fast roll rates** for thermalling
- **Inherent stability** in steep turns
- **Light-footedness** and precise control
- **"Grip"** feeling when centering thermals
- **Excellent weak condition performance**

### Flight Envelope
- Maintains controllability across entire speed range
- Safe and trustworthy with full water ballast
- Competitive edge in weak conditions
- Can retain water ballast longer than competitors

---

## Important Notes

### Safety Features
- Redundant engine systems (Neo version)
- Dual ignition system
- Automatic component switching
- CS-22 certification compliance

### Operational Considerations
1. **Water ballast significantly affects handling** during ground operations
2. **Electric systems require battery management** and charging
3. **15m tips completely redesigned** - not simple extensions
4. **Two fuselage options** affect empty weight and CG
