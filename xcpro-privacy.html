Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 April 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the XCPro mobile application, the XCPro website at www.xcpro.com.au, and related services operated under the XCPro name (together, XCPro, we, us, or our).

XCPro is an aviation and gliding-related application and service. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information and other data when you use XCPro.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • the XCPro Android app
  • the XCPro website
  • XCPro-hosted APIs, account features, live-sharing features, support channels, and related services

Some XCPro features may be optional, experimental, region-specific, or available only in certain app versions. Where that is the case, the relevant parts of this policy apply only if you use those features.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on the features you use, XCPro may collect or process the following categories of information.

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • your name, display name, handle, competition number, or similar profile details
  • your email address or other sign-in identifier
  • support messages, feedback, or other communications you send to us
  • information you submit when requesting support, account help, or data deletion

2.2 Location and flight-related data

Because XCPro is a navigation and flight-related application, we may collect or process:

  • precise location data from your device
  • altitude, speed, heading, course, and related movement data
  • waypoint, route, task, or navigation selections
  • live session information if you enable live tracking, live sharing, or spectator features
  • timestamps and technical metadata associated with flight activity

2.3 Sensor and device-generated flight data

Depending on your device, permissions, and enabled features, XCPro may process:

  • GPS data
  • barometer data
  • motion or orientation-related sensor data
  • traffic, weather, terrain, or airspace-related inputs used to provide XCPro functionality
  • imported or recorded flight logs and replay/simulation data

2.4 Account, social, and visibility data

If XCPro account or social features are available and you use them, we may collect or process:

  • account credentials or authentication tokens issued by a sign-in provider
  • profile settings and privacy preferences
  • follower, connection, request, and approval state
  • live visibility selections
  • share codes, watch links, or related access-control data

2.5 Purchase and subscription data

If XCPro offers paid features, subscriptions, or in-app purchases, we may receive limited transaction-related information such as subscription status, product tier, purchase validation status, and order or transaction identifiers.

XCPro does not receive your full payment card number from Google Play billing.

2.6 Technical, diagnostics, and security data

  • device model, operating system, app version, language, and time zone
  • app configuration, feature flags, and settings relevant to XCPro operation
  • crash data, performance data, and diagnostics
  • IP address, approximate region, request logs, and API access logs
  • fraud-prevention, abuse-prevention, and security event information

2.7 Website data

When you use the XCPro website, we or our hosting providers may automatically collect standard website/server data such as IP address, browser and device information, requested pages and referring URLs, timestamps, and server log information.

3. How we use information

  • provide XCPro’s core navigation, gliding, mapping, weather, traffic, safety, and flight-related features
  • provide live tracking, live sharing, spectator, follow, or account-related features where enabled
  • authenticate users and maintain account sessions
  • personalize profile, privacy, and configuration settings
  • validate purchases, subscriptions, and access to paid features
  • operate, secure, troubleshoot, monitor, and improve XCPro
  • respond to support requests and user communications
  • enforce our terms, prevent misuse, and protect users, XCPro, and the public
  • comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

4. When we share information

We do not sell personal information.

4.1 Service providers and infrastructure

We may share data with vendors or contractors that help us operate XCPro, such as hosting, storage, authentication, communications, monitoring, or support providers.

4.2 Feature providers

If a feature depends on a third-party service, relevant data may be shared with that service to deliver the feature you choose to use. Depending on the feature, this may include identity providers, mapping providers, weather providers, traffic/aviation data providers, telemetry or notification providers, and app store billing providers.

4.3 Live sharing and visibility features

If you enable a live session, share code, public watch link, follower-only visibility, or similar feature, your location, flight status, profile identity, or related live-session data may be visible to the people or audience allowed by that feature.

4.4 Legal, safety, and enforcement reasons

We may disclose information where reasonably necessary to comply with law, regulation, court order, or lawful request; enforce our legal rights or terms; detect, investigate, or prevent fraud, abuse, safety, or security issues; or protect users, third parties, or the public.

4.5 Business transfers

If XCPro or its assets are involved in a merger, acquisition, restructure, financing, or sale, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

5. Live tracking, social, and visibility controls

XCPro may offer optional live visibility or social features. If you use them:

  • information you choose to publish may become visible to other users or spectators
  • your visibility may depend on settings such as public, followers-only, or off
  • your profile may be searchable if you enable discoverability
  • follow, request, or relationship features may show limited identity and status data to other users

You are responsible for reviewing your settings before using live-sharing or public-visibility features.

6. Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to provide XCPro services and maintain account functionality, support flight history, logs, replay, troubleshooting, and safety-related features, maintain security and abuse-prevention records, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Retention periods vary depending on the type of data, the feature used, whether an account exists, whether a live session was created, and our legal obligations.

When information is no longer reasonably needed, we will take reasonable steps to delete it, de-identify it, or securely isolate it, unless we are required or permitted to retain it by law.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information against misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. These steps may include technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards such as access controls, authentication controls, logging, and encryption in transit where appropriate.

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International data handling

XCPro and its service providers may process or store information in Australia and in other countries where our infrastructure or providers operate.

Where information is disclosed to overseas recipients, we take reasonable steps to use reputable providers and appropriate safeguards, but data protection laws in other countries may differ from those in your country.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location and the way you use XCPro, you may have rights to access personal information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information, request deletion of your account or certain data, change privacy, discoverability, or visibility settings in the app where available, withdraw from optional features such as live sharing or public visibility, and disable permissions through your device settings.

If you want to make an access, correction, or deletion request, contact us using the details below.

10. Account and data deletion

If XCPro offers account creation or sign-in features, you may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@xcpro.com.au.

Where available, XCPro may also provide an in-app path to manage or delete account data.

We may need to verify your identity before actioning a deletion request. Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, technical, or record-keeping reasons.

11. Children

XCPro is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through XCPro without appropriate authorization.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to XCPro improperly, contact us and we will investigate and take appropriate action.

12. Third-party services and links

XCPro may contain links to third-party services, websites, or data sources. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices that we do not control.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make material changes, we may provide notice through the app, website, or other reasonable means. The updated version becomes effective when posted, unless stated otherwise.
