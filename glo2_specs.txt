Garmin GLO 2 specifications
---------------------------

Below you will find the product specifications and the manual specifications of the Garmin GLO 2.

The Garmin GLO 2 is a rechargeable GPS device that is equipped with LED indicators for easy use. It has an impressive operating time of up to 13 hours, ensuring long-lasting performance on the go. With a 10 Hz update rate, it provides fast and accurate location information for various outdoor activities. Measuring at a compact size, this device weighs only 60.1 grams and has dimensions of 17.8 millimeters in width, 77.2 millimeters in depth, and 41.9 millimeters in height. Its lightweight and portable design make it convenient to carry around during outdoor excursions. One notable feature of the Garmin GLO 2 is its WAAS capability, which enhances the accuracy and reliability of GPS positioning. It can achieve a position accuracy of 3 meters, making it suitable for precise navigation and tracking purposes. Furthermore, its GPS accuracy for velocity is 411 meters per second, providing accurate speed and direction information. The device is powered by a rechargeable battery with a capacity of 1150 mAh, ensuring sufficient power for extended use. Its LED indicators provide clear visual cues about various functions and status updates. Overall, the Garmin GLO 2 is a lightweight and portable GPS device with reliable performance. Its long operating time, WAAS capability, and accuracy in position and velocity measurements make it an ideal companion for outdoor enthusiasts and those in need of precise navigation. The durable construction and premium materials ensure its longevity and durability over time.

#### How can I pair my Garmin GLO 2 with my smartphone or tablet?

Pairing your Garmin GLO 2 with your smartphone or tablet is simple. You just need to go to the Bluetooth settings on your device, enable Bluetooth, and then search for available devices. Once you see the Garmin GLO 2 listed, select it to establish the pairing.

#### How do I check the battery level of my Garmin GLO 2?

To check the battery level of your Garmin GLO 2, you can access this information directly on your connected device. Usually, there is a battery indicator displayed on the screen of your smartphone or tablet when the device is paired with the Garmin GLO 2. Additionally, some navigation apps may also show the battery level within their settings or options.

#### How do I perform a firmware update on my Garmin GLO 2?

Keeping your Garmin GLO 2 up to date is crucial for optimal performance. To perform a firmware update, you can visit the Garmin website or use the Garmin Express software on your computer. This software will guide you through the process of connecting your device and updating its firmware to the latest version available.

#### How can I improve the GPS accuracy of my Garmin GLO 2?

If you are experiencing decreased GPS accuracy with your Garmin GLO 2, there are a few steps you can take to improve it. Ensure that your Garmin GLO 2 has a clear view of the sky, away from obstructions like buildings or dense foliage. Also, make sure you have a stable Bluetooth connection with your connected device, as interference or weak signals can impact accuracy. Lastly, you can try updating the GPS assistance data on your device, as this may enhance its performance.

#### Can I use my Garmin GLO 2 in different vehicles or should it be permanently installed?

The Garmin GLO 2 is designed to be portable and versatile, allowing you to use it across different vehicles without the need for permanent installation. You can simply place it on your dashboard, near a window, or in any location that provides a clear view of the sky. Thanks to its compact design and internal battery, you can easily move it between vehicles or even use it while hiking or cycling.
